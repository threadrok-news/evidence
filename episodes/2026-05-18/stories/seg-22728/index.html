Full Narration

A $10 billion lawsuit filed by Donald Trump against the IRS and the Treasury Department has been dropped. Trump moved to dismiss the case, which he launched in January over the leak of his tax returns, according to NPR. The dismissal comes alongside reports that a settlement is under consideration, though no deal has been publicly confirmed. Trump had demanded $10 billion in the original filing. Pay attention to whether a settlement is formally announced and on what terms. A negotiated resolution would close a case that centred on one of the most politically charged document leaks of the Trump era.
