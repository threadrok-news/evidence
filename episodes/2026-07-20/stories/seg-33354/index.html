Full Narration

A federal judge issued a temporary restraining order Monday, halting the Paramount Skydance takeover of Warner Bros. Discovery. The order came after state attorneys general requested the pause. Two of the largest media companies in the US cannot complete their merger while the order stands. The attorneys general raised concerns significant enough to secure a court order the same day they asked for one. Pay attention to whether the restraining order converts into a preliminary injunction — that would signal a prolonged legal fight rather than a short procedural delay. The deal, if it closes, would reshape the streaming and studio landscape. For now, it's frozen by a federal court, and the next hearing will set the timeline for what comes next.
