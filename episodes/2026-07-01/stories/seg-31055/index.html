The US will not renew the USMCA. The Trump administration announced Wednesday it's walking away from the current North American trade pact with Mexico and Canada — and pushing for a renegotiation. All 3 countries are now entering formal talks to replace the deal. The administration is seeking changes to the existing agreement, which has governed trade across North America since 2020. What this means for businesses on both sides of both borders depends entirely on how fast those negotiations move — and how far apart the 3 parties turn out to be. No timeline for a new deal has been set.
