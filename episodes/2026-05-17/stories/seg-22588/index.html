Full Narration

Senator Bill Cassidy of Louisiana has lost his Republican primary, failing to finish in the top 2. Cassidy was one of 7 Republican senators who voted to convict Donald Trump following the January 6, 2021 Capitol attack. Trump endorsed U.S. Representative Julia Letlow over Cassidy in the race. According to The Hill, Cassidy's conviction vote was the defining issue his opponents used against him throughout the campaign. Pay attention to which other Republican incumbents face primary challenges tied to their January 6 votes. Cassidy's loss gives those challengers a concrete data point heading into the rest of the 2026 primary cycle.
