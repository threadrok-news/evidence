Full Narration

A $1.8 billion DOJ fund created under the Trump administration is now on hold. A court blocked it, the Justice Department has agreed to abide by that ruling, and the AP reports Trump is now reconsidering the fund's future. The DOJ established it, a court halted it, and the administration said it will comply with the judicial order while Trump weighs his next move. The test is whether Trump moves to revive the fund through a different mechanism or lets it lapse under legal pressure.
