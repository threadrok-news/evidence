Full Narration

The Strait of Hormuz is closed. Iran's top joint military command announced the closure after Israel and Hezbollah exchanged fire, shutting off one of the world's most critical oil shipping lanes. The immediate trigger was the Israel-Hezbollah exchange of fire in Lebanon. Iran's armed forces cited that escalation as the basis for the shutdown. No timeline for reopening has been announced.
