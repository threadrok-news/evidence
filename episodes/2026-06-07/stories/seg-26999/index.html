Full Narration

President Trump walked out of a live NBC interview on Sunday before it was finished. The interview aired on Meet the Press, confirmed by a fact-check, and the walk-out came after an exchange with anchor Kristen Welker. During the interview, Trump repeatedly made false claims that the 2020 presidential election was rigged. Trump also directed criticism at what he called the 'crooked' press. BBC News reports he stormed out before the interview concluded. Pay attention to what comes next from the White House on press access. Walk-outs during broadcast interviews by a sitting president are rare, and this one aired in full before the exit occurred.
