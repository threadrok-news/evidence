Full Narration

WHO data shows 1,406 confirmed Ebola cases and 438 deaths in the Democratic Republic of Congo, with an experimental treatment trial now beginning in the east of the country. Snopes confirms the virus is actively spreading. DR Congo's government has banned mass gatherings in the capital as a containment measure. The treatment trial represents the first structured clinical intervention of this outbreak. The test is whether the trial can be conducted safely and at sufficient scale to generate usable data while the outbreak is still active. A 31% case fatality rate in the current WHO figures underscores what's at stake for affected communities.
