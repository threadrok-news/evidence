Full Narration

The criminal case against Kilmar Abrego Garcia is over. U.S. District Judge Waverly Crenshaw dismissed human smuggling charges against him on Friday, stating from the bench that Abrego Garcia was being prosecuted for political reasons. Abrego Garcia was mistakenly deported to El Salvador last year, a fact that's been central to his case since it became public. The dismissal removes the federal charges that had been filed after that deportation drew national attention. Pay attention to what comes next on the deportation question itself. The criminal case is closed, but Abrego Garcia's immigration status and whether he returns to the United States remains a separate, unresolved matter, according to NPR.
