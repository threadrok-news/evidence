Full Narration

Marine Le Pen will run for the French presidency in 2027. That's despite a conviction for embezzlement of public funds being upheld on appeal. A court ruling confirmed she is still legally permitted to stand as a candidate. Le Pen was sentenced to wear a court-ordered electronic monitor.
