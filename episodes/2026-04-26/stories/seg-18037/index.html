Full Narration

The suspect has been identified as Cole Tomas Allen, a California man arrested Saturday night at the scene. Snopes has verified his identity. Before the attack, Allen reportedly sent messages to family members stating he wanted to target administration officials. Trump attorney Todd Blanche said Allen could face charges of attempting to assassinate the president. The attempted assassination charge has not yet been formally filed, but Blanche indicated authorities are considering it. The question now is whether federal prosecutors bring that charge. The next expected development is a formal charging decision from the Justice Department.
